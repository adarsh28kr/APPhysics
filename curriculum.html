Week 1: Foundation Repair (Days 1-7)

Day 1
The Reckoning Review
Review every wrong answer from diagnostic exam WITH the student. For each, student writes: "I got this wrong because ___." Don't show answers — have them re-derive. Builds the self-awareness their confidence scores show they lack.
2 hrs
Day 2
Newton's Laws Boot Camp Newton
30 min: FBD drill — 10 different scenarios (incline+horizontal force, elevator, stacked blocks, conical pendulum)
30 min: Sign convention drill — T = M(g+a) up, T = M(g-a) down. 10 elevator problems alternating directions.
30 min: Friction direction — "Which way moving? Friction opposes that." 10 problems.
30 min: Two-body systems — "Find system acceleration first, then internal forces."
2 hrs
Day 3
Momentum Reboot Momentum
30 min: Elastic vs inelastic decision tree. Drill the formula: v1' = (m1-m2)/(m1+m2) * v1.
30 min: 2D momentum as vectors. "Perpendicular = Pythagorean theorem, NOT addition."
30 min: Rocket equation derivation. Practice: "Half mass expelled → delta-v = v_e ln(2)."
30 min: Center of mass calculations. Memorize: hemisphere 3R/8.
2 hrs
Day 4
Work & Energy Precision Energy
30 min: Spring PE drill. Write "1/2 k x^2" fifty times. Then 10 spring-launch problems.
30 min: Stability analysis. F = -dU/dx, check d2U/dx2: positive = stable, negative = unstable.
30 min: Power. "P = Fv. Lifting: F = mg. So P = mgv." 5 problems, always verify g is included.
1.5 hrs
Day 5
Oscillations & Gravitation Osc/Grav
30 min: Formula disambiguation chart — simple vs physical vs conical pendulum. Do 3 of each type. IDENTIFY the type first before writing any formula.
30 min: Orbits. v_orbit = sqrt(GM/r), v_escape = v_orbit * sqrt(2). Planet gravity: g = GM/R^2.
30 min: SHM energy. Total = 1/2 kA^2. At position x: KE = 1/2 k(A^2 - x^2). 5 problems.
1.5 hrs
Day 6
The Verification Habit Strategy
Most important session. Teach the 3-second check for every answer:
1. Units: Does my answer have the right units?
2. Limits: If mass → 0 or angle → 0/90°, does it make sense?
3. Magnitude: Is this a reasonable number?
Redo 10 wrong answers from diagnostic, applying all 3 checks each time.
1.5 hrs
Day 7
Rotation Refinement Rotation
30 min: Moment of inertia table: disk 1/2MR^2, sphere 2/5MR^2, shell 2/3MR^2, hoop MR^2, rod(end) 1/3ML^2. "More I → rolls slower. Solid beats hollow."
30 min: Angular momentum vs energy. "L conserved ≠ KE conserved. Skater: L same, KE up. Disk coupling: L same, KE down." 5 problems.
1 hr

Week 2: Integration & Exams (Days 8-15)

Day 8
Full Practice Exam #2 Exam
Timed, 45 min, no aids. Target: beat 18/35. Apply verification habit to every question.
2 hrs
Day 9
Error Comparison Review
Compare Exam 1 errors to Exam 2. Which mistakes eliminated? Which persisted? Persistent errors get red flag for extra drill.
1 hr
Day 10
Free Response Practice FRQ
3 full FRQs, timed 15 min each. Rule: "Write every equation, even if you can't solve. Partial credit is generous." Practice: diagram → principles → equations → solve → check limits.
2 hrs
Day 11
Mixed Problem Set Mixed
20 random problems across ALL topics (unlabeled). Forces student to IDENTIFY the topic before solving — critical for real exam which doesn't label questions.
1.5 hrs
Day 12
Weak Spot Blitz Targeted
Whatever is still below 60% from Exam 2, drill exclusively. Use "teach it back" method: student explains each concept aloud as if they're the teacher.
1 hr
Day 13
Full Practice Exam #3 Exam
Final timed exam. Target: 22+/35 (63%+). Apply all verification habits. Flag uncertain questions but still answer them.
2 hrs
Day 14
Light Review Review
Review formula sheet one last time. Do 5 easy confidence-building problems. Review common mistakes list from all exams. Review 3-second check habit.
45 min
Day 15
Exam Eve Rest
No new material. 3 warm-up problems (ones they've gotten right before). Review exam strategy: 75s per MC, skip and return, never leave blank, write every FRQ equation. Sleep by 10 PM.
30 min
